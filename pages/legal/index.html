1. Legal & Tenancy Compliance Guidelines

All private hostels housing students near Dedan Kimathi University of Technology must comply with local government regulations and university standards under the Directorate of Students' Welfare.

Tenancy Agreements & Security Deposits

  • Deposit Regulations: Under Kenya's Rent Restriction Act, landlord deposits are fully refundable upon vacancy, subject to normal wear-and-tear assessments.
  • Notice Period: Standard tenancy leases require a one-month written notice before vacation by either party.
  • Right to Privacy: Landlords must provide at least 24 hours notice prior to entering a student's room for maintenance or inspection.

Hostel Safety & Licensing Checklist

  • Security Measures: Properties must have functional biometric gates or 24-hour security guards, along with CCTV cameras in common entryways.
  • Sanitation & Water: Continuous clean water supply via boreholes or municipal connection, and a valid Public Health Certificate.
  • Fire Safety: Certified fire extinguishers placed on every floor and clear emergency exit routes.

2. Accessibility & Special Needs Assistance

DKUT is committed to ensuring that students with physical, visual, auditory, or medical challenges have equal access to secure and comfortable housing near the campus.

Priority Room Allocation

University policy ensures that students with verified special needs are given priority access to ground floor accommodation, shorter walking distances to gates, and accessible room setups.

Accessibility Infrastructure Requirements

  • Wheelchair Friendliness: Hostels certified for physical challenges must include step-free ramps, wide door entry frames (minimum 85cm), and safety handrails in bathrooms.
  • Auditory/Visual Supports: Ground floor placements, tactile markings, and visual safety/alarm lights.
  • Support Liaison: The DSW Special Needs office assists in placing students with disabilities in verified, compliant local hostels.
